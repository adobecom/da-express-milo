# Sitemap Indexes

Both sites that share this repo, Express (www.adobe.com/express/) and Color (color.adobe.com), need their own sitemap index.

## Express Sitemap Index
Located at `/express/sitemap-index.xml`

## Color Sitemap Index
Located at the root at `/sitemap-index.xml`

## Additional Information
- The individual sitemaps listed in the indexes are generated by AEM EDS and can be configured through the configuration service: https://www.aem.live/docs/admin.html#schema/SitemapConfig
